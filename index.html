Lycaa's Slave

Fetches material prices, so you know what it'll cost to craft.

Presets

the slave fetches; the slave does not explain itself.

ℹ️ What can this thing do?

One tool, no account, no backend — everything runs live in your browser, pinned to the Light data center. Pick anything below to open it; each opens as its own little window that you can drag around, and you can have as many open at once as you like.

📦 Craft Cost

Paste a wiki link or just type an item's name — the slave finds the recipe and prices every ingredient live, working out the cheapest way to actually buy each one (whole lots and all).

👗 Glamour

Paste an Eorzea Collection glamour link and get every piece named, priced, and explained — screenshots, exactly how to unlock each item, and a favorites list you can export.

🔍 Item Lookup

Look up any item to see exactly how it's obtained — crafted, gathered, dropped, bought — plus its current market price, even for things that aren't craftable.

🛒 Shopping List

Add several things to your basket and get one consolidated buy list, with duplicate ingredients across recipes summed instead of listed three times over.

💰 Profit Calc

Compares what an item costs to craft against what it actually sells for, so you know whether it's worth making before you buy a single material.

📊 Watchlist

Track specific ingredient prices over time, set low-price alerts (banner, sound, and browser notification), auto-refresh on a timer, and see your own little price-history charts.

🐴 Unlockables

Search mounts, minions, emotes, and fashion accessories — see what they look like, how rare they are, and exactly how to get them.

📜 Personal Stats

A tally of everything you've actually done here, plus a healthy amount of nonsense the slave made up about how far she's walked to the market board.

🧑‍🎤 Character Look

Found someone with a glamour you loved? Search their name + world, see their official Lodestone portrait, and save them so you can find that look again later.

✅ Daily Tasks

A recurring checklist — roulettes, beast tribes, Wondrous Tails, whatever you grind. Dailies uncheck themselves at the daily reset, weeklies at the weekly reset. Optional periodic reminders nudge you about anything still unfinished, with a snooze or skip-until-next-reset option.

📝 To-Do

Freeform goals that don't reset — "Eureka for the Anemos gear," "finish the Bozja resistance record," whatever. Add it, check it off when it's done.

🎰 Gold Saucer

Mini Cactpot solver — enter what you've scratched off and it works out the expected MGP for every line (row, column, diagonal), plus which cell to reveal next for the best odds.

Also worth knowing: the 🌍 button next to this one sets your home world, the basket floats in the top-right corner, and there's a dark mode toggle right here too — all three stay out of the way of your windows. Press Ctrl+K (⌘K on Mac) anywhere to pull up a quick search that jumps straight to a window, a saved character, a favorite, or a to-do item. And Personal Stats has a "Backup Everything" button that exports (or restores) every bit of data this tool remembers, in one file.

Heads up: you appear to have opened this straight from a file on your computer. A lot of browsers flatly refuse to let a local file talk to the internet, which means every lookup below may fail instantly no matter what — not the slave's fault, your browser's. Ask whoever gave you this to host it somewhere (GitHub Pages, etc.) instead of sending you the raw file.

📦 Craft Cost
How this works (tap to collapse)
  1. Think of the item or piece of gear you want to craft.
  2. Type its name, or paste its FFXIV wiki page URL, in the box below.
  3. Click "Look up recipe" — the slave finds the page, finds the recipe, and prices every ingredient on the Light data center automatically. No extra button to click.
  4. Click any ingredient's name in the results to see how it's actually obtained (gathered, bought, desynthed, etc) — and whether it's cheaper to craft it yourself.
  5. If the item can be crafted more than one way, switching the recipe dropdown re-prices it automatically too.

If it explodes, hit "Copy error for Lycaa" on the error card and send it over.
